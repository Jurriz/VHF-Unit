On this page, you'll find a few select example projects to demonstrate what you can achieve using the PeekyPokey. The examples also serve as tutorials and will help you getting started creating your own projects.

In many of the projects, you'll need one or two external and inexpensive parts available in most electronics stores and on-line shops. In general, you'll also need a breadboard and a set of jumper wires.
